More Than Just AI Chat

We've built a specialized travel planning system that combines AI intelligence with real-time data from authoritative sources — not just another chatbot.

Authoritative API Tools

While generic AI assistants rely on training data that can be outdated, we connect directly to official sources:

  • ✓ National Park Service API — Official park info, alerts, operating hours, and campground availability directly from NPS
  • ✓ Recreation.gov — Real campsite reservations and permit availability
  • ✓ Amadeus Travel API — Live flight prices, hotel rates, and car rental availability
  • ✓ Yelp Fusion — Current restaurant ratings, hours, and reservation links
  • ✓ Google Distance Matrix — Accurate drive times accounting for real road conditions
  • ✓ OpenChargeMap — EV charging station locations for Tesla and other EVs

Context Engineering

We don't just ask an AI to "plan a trip." We've engineered a sophisticated context system that makes every recommendation personalized:

  • ✓ Your Profile Drives Decisions — Tell us you're a "frugal traveler with kids" and every flight, hotel, and activity suggestion reflects that
  • ✓ Location-Aware — We detect your location and prioritize nearby parks, calculate accurate drive times, and find your nearest airports
  • ✓ Travel Distance Limits — Set a max travel distance and we'll only suggest destinations within your range
  • ✓ Preference Memory — Your airline, hotel chain, and car rental preferences are remembered throughout your planning session

Privacy-First Design

Your travel preferences are yours alone:

  • ✓ Ephemeral Profile — Your profile is stored only on your device, never on our servers
  • ✓ No Account Required — Start planning immediately without creating an account or sharing personal data
  • ✓ Session-Based Context — Your conversation context helps personalize recommendations but isn't stored long-term
Your data stays on your device

  • ◐ State Park Data — Expanding beyond national parks to include state parks, regional parks, and public lands across all 50 states
