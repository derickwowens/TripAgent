Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Adventure Agent ("TripAgent", "We", "Us", or "Our") collects, uses, and protects Your information when You use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting Your privacy and being transparent about the minimal data We collect.

Information We Collect

Location Data (Optional)

With Your permission, We may access Your device's location to:

  • Show nearby National Parks and points of interest
  • Provide location-relevant travel recommendations

You can enable or disable location access at any time through Your device settings. The app works without location access.

Conversation Data

When You chat with the AI assistant:

  • Your messages are sent to our servers to generate responses
  • Conversations are stored locally on Your device
  • We use OpenAI's API to process messages - see OpenAI's Privacy Policy

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect Your email address
  • We do not use analytics or tracking software
  • We do not sell or share Your data with third parties for advertising

How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Provide the Service: Generate AI-powered travel recommendations for National Parks
  • Improve the App: Fix bugs and enhance features based on general usage patterns

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect Your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

Conversation data is stored locally on Your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to National Park Service websites, booking sites, and other third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
