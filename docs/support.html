Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

Simply type your question about National Parks in the chat. Ask about hiking trails, camping, fees, best times to visit, or anything else about the 63 US National Parks!

Why does the app ask for my location?

Location access is optional. If enabled, the app can show you nearby National Parks and provide more relevant recommendations. You can use the app without location access.

How do I delete my conversations?

Conversations are stored locally on your device. To delete them, you can swipe left on a conversation to delete it, or clear the app's data through your device settings.

Is my data private?

Yes! We don't require accounts or collect personal information. Conversations are stored on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app isn't working properly

Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • If issues persist, contact us below
